TextToVid AI is a desktop application that turns plain-language text prompts into AI-generated videos — all on your own hardware, with no cloud services involved. If you've ever wanted to visualize a scene, prototype a video concept, or experiment with generative media without signing up for a subscription or sharing your data with a third party, TextToVid AI was built for you.

## What is TextToVid AI?

TextToVid AI bridges the gap between written imagination and moving visuals. You write a text prompt describing the scene or video you have in mind — the setting, mood, lighting, motion, and any other details you want to include. TextToVid AI passes that prompt to a locally installed GGUF language model, which interprets your description and drives a frame generation pipeline. Those frames are assembled in sequence, processed for temporal consistency, and rendered into a finished video file that you can export and use however you like.

The entire process happens on your machine. There are no API calls to external servers, no content filters imposed by a third-party provider, and no waiting in a cloud queue. The speed and quality of generation depend on your hardware, but even a mid-range machine with a dedicated GPU can produce short videos in a matter of minutes.

## Why Local AI?

Running AI on your own hardware rather than in the cloud comes with meaningful advantages:

- **Privacy by default.** Your prompts, your generated content, and any reference images you provide never leave your machine. There is no telemetry, no training data collection, and no remote logging.
- **No subscription costs.** TextToVid AI is a one-time install. You choose which GGUF models to download, and many high-quality models are freely available. There are no per-generation fees, no monthly plans, and no credit systems.
- **Works completely offline.** Once the app and your chosen model are installed, you need no internet connection to generate videos. Work on a plane, in a remote location, or in any air-gapped environment.
- **Full control over your model.** You decide which model to use, when to update it, and how to configure it. You are not subject to a provider changing, deprecating, or rate-limiting the model underneath you.

## System Requirements

Before installing TextToVid AI, make sure your machine meets the following specifications:

| Component | Minimum | Recommended |
|-----------|---------|-------------|
| **Operating System** | Windows 10, macOS 12 Monterey, Ubuntu 20.04 | Windows 11, macOS 14 Sonoma, Ubuntu 22.04+ |
| **RAM** | 16 GB | 32 GB or more |
| **Storage** | 5 GB for the app + space for models (4–20 GB each) | SSD with 50 GB+ free for models and output |
| **GPU** | Optional (CPU generation is supported) | NVIDIA with 8 GB+ VRAM, AMD RX 6000+, or Apple Silicon |
| **CPU** | 4-core x86-64 or Apple Silicon | 8-core or higher |

<Note>
  GPU acceleration is strongly recommended for practical generation speeds. Without a GPU, generating even a short 5-second video at low resolution can take 20–60 minutes depending on your CPU. With a supported GPU, the same video typically takes 2–5 minutes.
</Note>
