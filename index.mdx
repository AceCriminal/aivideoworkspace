Whether you're a filmmaker, content creator, or just curious about generative AI, TextToVid AI gives you full creative control with zero cloud dependencies, no subscriptions, and no data leaving your computer.

## How It Works

Getting from a blank screen to a finished video takes just four steps. Follow the onboarding flow below to get up and running quickly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install TextToVid AI">
    Download the installer for your platform — Windows `.exe`, macOS `.dmg`, or Linux `.AppImage` — and run it on your machine. No internet connection is required after download.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download a GGUF Model">
    TextToVid AI uses local GGUF-format language models to interpret your prompts and drive video generation. Download a compatible model from HuggingFace and place it in the app's `models` directory.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write Your Prompt">
    Open TextToVid AI and type a description of the scene or video you want to create. Be as descriptive as you like — the more detail you provide, the more precisely the model can interpret your vision.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate Your Video">
    Click **Generate**, choose your duration and resolution settings, and let the model do the work. When generation is complete, export your video as an MP4 or another supported format.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key Features

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Fully Offline" icon="wifi-slash">
    Every part of the generation pipeline runs locally. No data is ever sent to external servers, and no internet connection is required during use.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Local GGUF Models" icon="brain">
    Use any compatible GGUF-format model from HuggingFace or your own library. Swap models freely to experiment with different styles and capabilities.
  </Card>
  <Card title="GPU Accelerated" icon="microchip">
    Take advantage of NVIDIA CUDA, AMD ROCm, or Apple Metal to dramatically speed up video generation on supported hardware.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Batch Generation" icon="layer-group">
    Queue multiple prompts and let TextToVid AI process them back-to-back, freeing you to focus on other work while your videos are generated.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Custom Prompts" icon="pen-to-square">
    Full control over your prompt text, negative prompts, and generation parameters means every video can be tuned to your exact creative intent.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Multiple Export Formats" icon="file-export">
    Export finished videos as MP4, WebM, or GIF. Individual frames can also be saved as PNG sequences for use in external editing tools.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
